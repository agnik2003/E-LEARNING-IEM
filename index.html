Gender Predictor Application

Gender prediction using name involves using the characteristics of a person's name to determine their gender. While it can be accurate in many cases, it is not always reliable due to the increasing popularity of gender-neutral names and the complexity of gender identity. Names are often associated with gender, and many cultures have naming conventions that reflect this association. For example, names ending in "a" are often associated with females in some cultures, while names ending in "o" are often associated with males in others.
